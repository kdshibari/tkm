    Safe Words

    Safe words are essential for maintaining consent and communication during BDSM activities. Below are some expamples of safe words:

    • Green - Everything is okay, continue.
    • Yellow - Slow down or check-in.
    • Red - Stop immediately.

    Make sure both partners agree on safe words before starting.

    Aftercare

    Aftercare ensures both partners feel supported post-scene. Below are some expamples of how aftercare may include:

    • Water, snacks, or a blanket.
    • Cuddling or holding each other.
    • Discussing the scene and feelings.
    • Taking care of marks or injuries.

    Plan aftercare with your partner beforehand.
